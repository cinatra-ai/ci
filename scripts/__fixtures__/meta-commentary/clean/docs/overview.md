# Example integration

The Example integration connects your Example workspace to Cinatra so agents can
read and act on your data. Once installed, a connector exposes Example's records
as skills any connected agent can call as slash commands.

## What you can do

- Search records and open them from a chat.
- Create and update items through natural language.
- Share reusable prompts across your team.

Every action runs with the permissions you grant during install, and nothing
leaves your workspace unless you ask for it.
